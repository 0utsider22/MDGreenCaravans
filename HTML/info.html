Who Are We?

MDGreen Caravan Sales is a small family business located in Carrum Downs, Victoria.

Our Mission

At MDGreen Caravan sales our mission is simple:
To provide high quality, affordable second hand caravans to Australian families and travellers.

Why Buy From Us?

Drawing from over 30 years of combined experience in the maintenance, repair and sale of caravans, you can be sure that any caravan purchased from MDGreen Caravans has been thoroughly inspected and serviced to the highest of professional standards.
All MDG caravans come with 9+ months Victorian registration meaning you can hook on, load up and hit the road with the sort of confidence you can't get with a private sale.
